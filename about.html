About
KA-playlist is a free web-app meant to help educators to help their students prepare for class with Khan Academy. It is based on the Khan Academy api.

The idea behind this tool is that you can create a custom curriculum of prerequisites that will ensure incoming students are prepared to take on your course material.
Examples
Teaching astronomy? Maybe your students need to brush up on some rudimentary physics or chemistry concepts so that you can focus on actually teaching astronomy.

Teaching poly-sci? Maybe there's a good history lesson that your students can familiarize themselves with before you meet to discuss present times.

Your custom playlist can help students to prepare at home for more meaningful discussions during the more precious limited class time.
Why
This concept was developed when a design student wanted to take the acclaimed Stanford machine learning course online. He needed to brush-up on math, but what parts of math? Khan academy offers thousands of videos organized on math but he didn’t know where to begin.

After crawling Khan Academy for some time, I found in the forums that many educators were looking for this...
